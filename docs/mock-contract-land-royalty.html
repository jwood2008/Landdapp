LAND ROYALTY AGREEMENT

Mineral & Surface Rights Royalty Payment Contract

Contract No. LRA-2025-0042
1. PARTIES

This Land Royalty Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2025, by and between:

Landowner / Royalty Obligor: Heartland Energy Resources LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, with principal offices at 1200 Prairie View Drive, Suite 400, Oklahoma City, OK 73102 ("Obligor").

Royalty Holder / Beneficiary: Wood Land Holdings Inc., and its designated token holders, assignees, and successors ("Beneficiary").

2. PROPERTY DESCRIPTION

The royalty interest applies to the following described real property:

A 640-acre parcel located in Section 14, Township 12N, Range 8W, Carter County, Oklahoma, including all mineral rights, surface use rights, and subsurface extraction rights (the "Property").

3. ROYALTY PAYMENT TERMS
Annual Royalty Amount: $120,000.00 (One Hundred Twenty Thousand US Dollars)
Payment Frequency: Quarterly ($30,000.00 per quarter)
Payment Due Date: 15th day of the first month of each calendar quarter (January 15, April 15, July 15, October 15)
Currency: USD (United States Dollars)
Payment Method: Digital settlement via XRPL token distribution or wire transfer
4. AGREEMENT TERM
Effective Date: March 1, 2025
Expiration Date: February 28, 2035
Term Duration: 10 years, with option to renew
5. ANNUAL ESCALATION

The annual royalty amount shall increase by 3.5% per year, compounded annually, beginning on the first anniversary of the Effective Date. This escalation reflects projected increases in land value and extraction yields.

Escalation Rate: 3.5% per annum
Escalation Type: Annual percentage increase, compounded
6. ROYALTY BASIS

The royalty payments are derived from the productive use of the Property, including but not limited to: mineral extraction operations, surface lease arrangements with agricultural tenants, wind/solar energy installations, and any other revenue-generating activities conducted on or beneath the Property.

7. DISTRIBUTION TO TOKEN HOLDERS

The Beneficiary may fractionalize its royalty interest via blockchain-based security tokens. Royalty payments received shall be distributed to token holders proportionally based on their ownership percentage, subject to applicable platform reserves and regulatory requirements.

8. DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

Failure to make any royalty payment within 30 days of the due date shall constitute a default. Upon default, the Beneficiary may pursue all available legal remedies, including but not limited to acceleration of all remaining payments under this Agreement.

9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Oklahoma.
