---

"In the last 18 months, Big Tech has spent over $150 billion building AI data centers. Guess what they're building them on?

Land.

Microsoft, Google, Amazon — they're buying rural and agricultural land at record pace. Meanwhile, global food demand is projected to rise 50% by 2050. ### SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM (15 sec)

"Land is the oldest asset class on earth — over $30 trillion globally — but it trades like it's 1950. Paper deeds. Six-month closings. $500,000 minimums. It's illiquid, inaccessible, and opaque."

---

### SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION (20 sec)

"TierraDex tokenizes land and agricultural assets on the XRP Ledger. We fractionalize ownership into tokens, so anyone can buy a stake in land assets for as little as $50. You get real yield — revenue from leases, rent, and harvest — sent directly to your wallet. And you can trade your tokens anytime on the built-in XRPL DEX. No lockups."

---

### SLIDE 4: WHY XRPL (15 sec)

"Why build on XRP? Because it was designed for this. 3-to-5-second settlement. Near-zero fees. A built-in decentralized exchange for secondary trading. And no smart contract exploits — token functionality lives at the protocol level. XRPL was purpose-built for real-world asset tokenization."

---

### SLIDE 5: HOW IT WORKS (20 sec)

"Here's how it works. An issuer onboards a land asset with legal documentation and appraisal. Fractional ownership tokens are minted on XRPL. Investors buy on our marketplace or the DEX. And here's the magic — our AI oracle monitors real production data and automatically distributes revenue to token holders on-chain. No middleman. No manual payouts."

"Three mega-trends are converging right now. The AI infrastructure boom is driving unprecedented demand for land. Global food needs are rising 50% by 2050. And BlackRock and BCG project $16 trillion in tokenized assets by 2030. Land sits at the intersection of all three."
